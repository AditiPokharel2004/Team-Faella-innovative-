Basic Manners

Please and Thank You

Always say "please" when asking for something and "thank you" when you receive it.

Excuse Me

Use "excuse me" when you need to interrupt or get someone's attention

Share

Share your toys, books, and other things with others

Listen

Pay attention when someone is speaking and wait your turn to talk

Apologize

Say "I'm sorry" if you accidentally hurt someone or make a mistake.

Respect

Treat others with kindness and respect their feelings and belongings.

Good Table Manners

Use utensils properly, chew with your mouth closed, and don't talk with your mouth full.

Clean Up

Tidy up after yourself and put things back where they belong.

Say "You're Welcome"

Respond with “you're welcome” when someone thanks you.
