Let's Learn Communication Skills!

1. Listening

Listening is paying attention to what others are saying. It's important to listen carefully to understand them.

2. Speaking Clearly

Speaking clearly helps others understand you better. Use a calm and friendly voice.

3. Asking Questions

Asking questions shows that you are interested in what others are saying. It's a great way to learn more!

4. Saying Thank You

Always remember to say "Thank you" when someone helps you. It makes everyone feel happy!

Practice these skills every day to become a great communicator!
